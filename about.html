My Story

Food that healed me taught me how to cook

I didn't set out to become a chef. I set out to feel better. After years of dealing with fatigue and inflammation, I started paying attention to what I was putting in my body — and everything changed.

I discovered that eating plant-based, whole foods wasn't a sacrifice. It was a revelation. Flavors were brighter, energy was steadier, and food stopped feeling like a source of guilt and started feeling like joy.

I trained myself by cooking obsessively — experimenting with naturally sweetened desserts, perfecting whole-grain bowls, learning to coax deep flavor from vegetables. Eventually, friends started asking me to cook for them. Then strangers. Then it became this.

Nourishment shouldn't be a chore. When food is made with care, eating well becomes the most natural thing in the world.

Skylar  ·  Founder

What I Believe

The principles that guide every dish

Naturally Sweet
